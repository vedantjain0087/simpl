Why Simpl ?

We believe and you will relate as you read this up. Your college does not teaches you what the industry want out of you. The education system is outdated and no more serves as a tool to learn what matters. We believe everyone has the right to learn what is actually right to learn the skills which matter, the tools which matters, the languages which matters, the frameworks which matters, the platform which matters.

How Simpl ?

At Simpl, we connect the students who want to learn with the people who can teach them what they want, what they should and more than teachers they become friends and advisors for your professional life. We are trying to bridge the gap one between a bustling mind in college to ones who have seen it, done it and proved it.

What Simpl ?

We offer a set of tools for one to learn what they want from what they want. Our courses are 2 weeks skill enhancement programs in which the student is given one on one mentoring and lectures. The skills earned are then tested through programming assignments and projects. A nominal fee is charged from the student for the time of instructor, eveything else is free and open. Simpl also provides active community groups and discussions on whatsapp as well as on telegram. Join now, its free, its Simpl.
